CAUSE OF MENTAL HEALTH

Stress in everyday life is necessary to some . But if you face a stressful situation every day,it can affect your mental and physical health.There can be various things that cause stress.It can be from a small matter to a big problem.If these issues are understood individually, where they are resolved, it can help reduce stress.The following are the topics that cause stress.

Childhood abuse
trauma or neglect
Social isolation or loneliness
Eocial disadvantage
Bereavement
Stress due to work
Family problems
Overthinking
Unsuccessful marriage
